Privacy Policy for Super Number Bubbles
Effective Date: October 29th, 2025

Super Number Bubbles (“the App”) is a casual mobile game developed and published by an independent developer (“we,” “us,” or “our”). 
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect
Super Number Bubbles itself does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly.
However, the App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, which may collect certain data automatically for the purpose of showing and measuring ads.
This may include:
- Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
- Approximate location (based on IP address)
- App interaction and usage data
To learn more, see Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How Information Is Used
Data collected by Google AdMob is used to:
- Display and measure in-app advertisements
- Personalize ads (if permitted by the user’s device settings)
- Improve ad performance and relevance
We do not access or control this data directly.

3. Opt-Out and Controls
You can limit or opt out of ad personalization at any time:
- On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy → Tracking and adjust permissions.
- You may also reset your device’s Advertising Identifier.

4. Children’s Privacy
Super Number Bubbles is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
